Since the beginning of 2015, Golden: Silent Improvised Stories has been presenting wordless shows on on every improv stage in Austin, and at festivals at home and abroad. Drawing inspiration from classic silent films, and incorporating elements of everything from traditional narrative improvisation to clowning and mime, we have worked non-stop to create a style, method, and vocabulary of silent and physical performance, and now, for the first time, we are offering our knowledge and training to you!

This series of workshops is intended to explore the breadth and depth of silent performance techniques that we've developed and discovered since our inception as a Hideout Student Mainstage. Under the direction of Austin improv veterans Marc Majcher and Jayme Ramsay, Golden has rapidly evolved into an ever-growing ensemble of stellar physical performers, each player bringing something valuable and unique to our collective pool of knowledge.

We've been pulling our repertoire apart into its component parts for your enjoyment and edification, and have developed a jam-packed six-week curriculum for you, including:

  • Effortlessly bringing vibrant characters to life!
  • Finding the fun in a scene, and pushing those hot spots to their limits!
  • How to do crisp, precise, and intentional spacework!
  • Participating in a meaningful conversation without speaking a word!
  • Wordlessly building vivid environments on stage!
  • Telling a cohesive story without speaking, in sixty seconds or sixty minutes!

This series of workshops will be held at the new Hideout Studios space (2505 E 6th, Unit 3C) from 4pm-7pm on Sundays in March and April. They are extremely reasonably priced at $40 for each three-hour session, or $200 for all six workshops - almost twenty hours of instruction, for just ten dollars an hour! To ensure that everyone gets enough time to play and learn, we're putting a cap of twelve students per workshop, and we will likely be arranging a student showcase at the end of the run, so you can show off everything that you've learned!

Everyone in Golden is extremely excited to share everything that we've learned with you! We've gotten a great response already - these workshops are expected to fill up quickly, so grab your spot while you can!

Thanks, and we look forward to playing with you all!

Spacework and Environment

with Suzanne Link and Tyler Lane

Sun 3/6 4pm-7pm - $40

Defining the who, what, when, and where is a challenge in any improv scene. Effective spacework can establish more than just what your character is holding in their hand. By developing and sharpening your spacework skills, you can quickly define character, environment, and provide the audience with a fuller theatrical experience. In just three hours, we will provide easy tips, tricks, and tools that you can immediately put into practice to take your improv to next level.

Physical Game and Heightening

with Brandon Martin and Jayme Ramsay

Sun 4/3 4pm-7pm - $40

How do you play when you can't talk? Where's the fun? In this workshop, participants will learn to find a game and have fun with while also grasping with the fact that you don't have to say anything. We will learn your basic three-slap routine and how to apply the idea and the thinking behind it to your silent scenework.
